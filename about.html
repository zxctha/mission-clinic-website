Our Mission

Our Mission is to provide fast, accurate and precise laboratory results, with passion, patience, commitment, excellence, and quality service, that will be made available for the community. Provide affordable clinical laboratory tests that will reach out everyon, regardless of their social status. To help and aide our Public Health Physicians restoring the health of the community.

Our Vision

As Christ's Servants, our vision is to build a community that offers them a safe place with hope, love, peace and encouragement to one another. We envision to grow and reach out more individual that needs our services in different areas of the country.
